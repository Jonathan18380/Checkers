Checkers Rules:

General rules-

  • 1. The board is 8x8.
  • 2. 2 players- one has dark pieces, one has light pieces.
  • 3. Players alternate turns.
  • 4. A player may not move an opponents piece.
  • 5. Only the dark square are used.
  • 6. Capturing is mandatory

Men-

  • 1. Uncrowned men move one step diagonally forwards, and in order to capture an opponents piece it needs to move two consecutive block in the same line jumping over the opponents piece.
  • 2. Multiple enemy pieces can be captured in a single turn provided this is done by successive jumps made by a single piece.
  • 3. Men can jump forward not backwards.
  • 4. You have to capture the opponents pieces.
  • 5. If you do not capture the opponents piece that same piece will be disqualified.

Kings-

  • 1. A man is promoted to king when it reaches to the farthest row.
  • 2. Powers-
  • a. Can move backwards.
  • b. Can capture backwards.
  • c. Can make successive jumps in a single turn.
  • d. The king can stop at any square after a jump in the same line.
